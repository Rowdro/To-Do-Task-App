Privacy & Policy

To-Do-Task Manager App ‚Äì Privacy Policy

Effective date: 06 December 2025

1. Information we collect

We only store the data you explicitly enter (tasks, name, email, profile picture). Everything is kept locally in your browser's IndexedDB / localStorage; nothing is sent to our servers.

2. How we use your information

Your tasks are displayed, filtered and reminded entirely on your device. We do not share, sell or analyse your data.

3. Cookies / "Remember me"

The "Remember me" option saves your email address in localStorage so you don't have to type it again. You can clear it by unticking the box or clearing your browser data.

4. Third-party services

Google-sign-in is handled by Firebase Authentication. Their respective privacy policies apply when you choose that option.
